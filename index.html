Information Collection and Use

AddictOut is built as a Freemium app. We do not require account registration or collect personal identifiable information like your name or email.

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. This includes device info, IP address, and advertising IDs.

Data Storage & Deletion

All progress data (streaks, history, and goals) is stored locally on your device. We do not store this data on our servers. You can delete all your data at any time using the "Reset All Progress" option in the app settings.
